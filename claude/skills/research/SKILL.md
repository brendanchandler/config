# Research Skill

Research $0 in depth and write a comprehensive report.

## Research Process

1. **Explore Structure**: Use Glob to understand the directory layout and file organization
2. **Analyze Files**: Read key files to understand purpose, architecture, and implementation
3. **Identify Patterns**: Look for design patterns, dependencies, and relationships between modules
4. **Document Findings**: Note important concepts, algorithms, and design decisions

## Output Format

Write a detailed report and save it to $0/README.md:

- **Purpose**: What does this component/module do?
- **Architecture**: How is it organized? What are the main components?
- **Key Files**: List important files with their purposes
- **Dependencies**: What does it depend on? What depends on it?
- **Design Patterns**: What patterns does it use?
- **Implementation Details**: How does it work? Any algorithms or techniques of note?
- **Examples**: Show how it's used

Be thorough. A reader should understand the component deeply after reading your report.
